1. First input section requires you to put in your current WAM.

2. Second input section requires you to put in how many subjects you have completed so far.

3. Third input section requires you to put in your goal WAM.

4. Fourth input section requires you to put in how many subjects would've been completed by the time you want our WAM to be at your goal.

e.g :

Bob is currently sitting on 69 WAM with 11 subjects completed and wants to know what grades he needs to earn on every subject on average to reach 80 WAM by the time he finishes 18 subjects in total.

1. 69

2. 11

3. 80

4. 18
